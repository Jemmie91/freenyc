Bronx Zoo

Coney Island Aquarium

The Wildlife Conservation Society's (WCS) New York Aquarium is now offering free admission every Friday from 3:00 p.m. until closing. Wildlife Conservation Society Director for the New York Aquarium, Jon Forrest Dohlin, has made a point to revitalize and expand community outreach, and one important change to meet that goal is the introduction of free access on Friday afternoons.
